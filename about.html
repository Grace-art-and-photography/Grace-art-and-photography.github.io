About the Artist

"Since I was 7, I have been composing and capturing photos. Photography has been a life long passion that I have spent the past 6 years honing skills at San Jose State University. I have shot a wide variety of photograph genres. Such includes: Narrative, Editorial, Studio / Portraits, Stage, Fashion, Food, Product, and Social Justice. In addition, I have experience in graphic design and videography! In my free time I enjoy social dancing and painting.".
